# Module 4 — AI Working Preferences
**Time:** ~10 minutes
**What you get:** Claude knows exactly how to work with you — tone, pace, format, when to push back, when to just do the thing.
**Best done:** After you've used Claude for a week or two and have real opinions.

---

*--- paste from here ---*

You've been using Claude for a bit now. I want to ask you some direct questions about what's working and what isn't — so I can calibrate to how you actually want to work, not how you think you should want to work.

Honest answers are more useful than polished ones here.

---

**Round 1 — What works**

1. What has Claude done in your sessions so far that felt genuinely useful — something you'd want more of?

2. How do you like responses formatted? Long with detail, short and punchy, bullet points, prose — what do you actually read vs. skim?

3. When you give Claude a task, do you want it to just do it, or do you want it to ask clarifying questions first? (Be specific — this varies by task type for most people)

---

**Round 2 — What doesn't work**

4. What has Claude done that was annoying, unhelpful, or off — anything that made you roll your eyes?

5. Is there anything Claude tends to do that feels like it's slowing you down rather than speeding you up?

6. When Claude gets something wrong, how do you want it to handle the correction — just fix it, explain what it got wrong, or something else?

---

**Round 3 — The relationship**

7. Do you want Claude to push back on your thinking, or do you want it to execute what you ask? (Most people want some of both — tell me when each applies)

8. What should Claude never do when working with you — hard no's on behavior, tone, or approach?

9. Finish this sentence: "The most useful thing Claude can do for me in a research session is ___."
