# Module 5 — Current Project
**Time:** ~10 minutes
**What you get:** Claude is fully briefed on what you're working on right now — no re-explaining every session.
**When to run:** Start of each new project. This one gets refreshed, not accumulated.

Let's get me fully briefed on what you're working on right now. This is the "current project" layer — it changes with each project, so you'll redo this one more often than the others.

---

**Round 1 — The brief**

1. What's the project? Give me a one-sentence description and then as much context as you want.

2. What's the core research question — what are you actually trying to find out?

3. Who are the participants — who are you researching, and how are you getting access to them?

---

**Round 2 — Where you are**

4. What stage are you in right now? (Planning, recruiting, in the field, synthesizing, reporting, handoff — whatever's true)

5. What have you done so far? What data do you already have?

6. What's the next thing you need to do?

---

**Round 3 — The stakes**

7. Who commissioned this research and what do they expect to do with it?

8. What's the timeline — when does this need to be done?

9. What would a great outcome look like? And what are you worried about?
